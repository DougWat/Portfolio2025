FORVRMood 2025 Redesign & Product Launch

My client, FORVRMood, wanted to redesign their digital storefront ahead of a product launch.

Along with that redesign, they needed to leverage advanced shopify features to communicate the value of their new products.

Dynamic SVG Fragrance Display

The client wanted to highlight the head, heart, and base notes of their new fragrance line with images and descriptions in a compact format.

This dynamic SVG allows the client to highlight up to 8 notes, and is fully customizable via the shopify admin storefront.

Simplfied Cart Drawer

The client wanted the benefits of a dynamic cart drawer, without needing to pay for a complicated third party app.

This cart drawer dyanamically updates cart contents, quantity, and price, in addition to other features such as product sudgestions, a free-shipping meter, and gift/promotional CTAs
